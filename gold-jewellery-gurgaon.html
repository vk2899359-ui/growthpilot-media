Gold Purity & BIS Hallmark Guarantee

At Auric Jewels, every piece of gold jewellery in Gurgaon carries the BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) hallmark — India's official certification of gold purity. This ensures you receive exactly the purity you pay for, with no compromise.

Understanding Gold Purity

22 Karat (916): Contains 91.6% pure gold. The standard for most Indian jewellery — ideal for necklaces, bangles, earrings, and traditional pieces. This is the most popular choice for gold jewellery in Gurgaon due to its rich colour and durability.

18 Karat (750): Contains 75% pure gold mixed with other metals for added strength. Perfect for diamond-studded pieces, daily-wear rings, and contemporary designs where durability is essential.

14 Karat (585): Contains 58.5% pure gold. Ideal for lightweight daily-wear pieces and fashion jewellery that needs to withstand everyday use.

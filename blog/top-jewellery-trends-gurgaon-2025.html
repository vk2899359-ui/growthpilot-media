Top 10 Jewellery Trends in Gurgaon for 2025

From minimalist diamonds to bold gold statements, discover the jewellery trends defining style in Gurgaon and across India. Curated by the design team at Auric Jewels.

The jewellery landscape in Gurgaon is evolving rapidly. Today's buyers are more design-conscious, quality-aware, and expressive than ever before. At Auric Jewels, we're seeing a fascinating blend of traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary global aesthetics. Here are the 10 jewellery trends dominating Gurgaon in 2025.

1 Minimalist Diamond Jewellery

The "less is more" philosophy has taken Gurgaon by storm. Delicate diamond solitaire pendants, single-stone studs, and thin diamond bands are the new everyday luxury. Young professionals in Cyber City and Golf Course Road are opting for refined, understated pieces that transition seamlessly from office to evening. At Auric Jewels, our minimalist diamond collection starts from just ₹25,000 — proving that luxury doesn't require excess.

2 Layered Gold Necklaces

Stacking multiple gold chains of varying lengths creates a personalised, textured look that's both traditional and modern. The trend involves mixing fine chains with slightly chunkier ones, often incorporating small pendants or charms. In Gurgaon, we're seeing buyers mix 22K yellow gold chains with 18K rose gold pieces for a contemporary two-tone effect. This trend works beautifully for both casual brunches and festive occasions.

3 Coloured Gemstone Rings

Emeralds, rubies, and sapphires are making a spectacular comeback. Coloured gemstone engagement rings, inspired by royal jewellery, are gaining popularity among Gurgaon's fashion-forward couples looking for something unique. Emeralds set in 18K gold are particularly sought after, combining the richness of green with warm gold tones. At Auric Jewels, our gemstone collection features certified natural stones in bespoke settings.

4 Vintage-Inspired Bridal Sets

Brides in Gurgaon are looking back in time for inspiration. Art deco diamond patterns, Victorian-era milgrain detailing, and Mughal-inspired polki sets are in high demand. These vintage-inspired pieces carry the grandeur of heritage while being crafted with modern precision and certification standards. Our bridal studio at Auric Jewels creates custom vintage-inspired sets that blend old-world charm with contemporary craftsmanship.

5 Statement Ear Cuffs

Ear cuffs — jewellery that wraps around the outer ear without requiring piercings — are the breakout accessory of 2025. Available in gold, diamond-studded, and mixed-metal versions, ear cuffs add edge and personality. Gurgaon's young professionals love them for their versatility: bold enough for a night out, subtle enough for daily wear. Diamond ear cuffs from Auric Jewels start at ₹35,000.

6 Personalised Name Jewellery

Custom name necklaces, initial pendants, and date-engraved rings are trending heavily in Gurgaon. The desire for personalisation reflects a broader shift towards meaningful, sentimental jewellery. At Auric Jewels, our engraving and custom design services allow you to add names, dates, or personal messages to any piece — transforming fine jewellery into a deeply personal keepsake.

7 Mixed Metal Stacking Rings

Gone are the days of matching all your metals. In 2025, Gurgaon's style-conscious buyers are stacking rings in different metals — white gold with yellow gold, rose gold with platinum, plain bands with diamond-set ones. The mix-and-match approach creates a unique, personalised hand that reflects individual style. Our ring collection includes stackable bands designed to be combined in endless combinations.

8 Oversized Gold Hoops

Large gold hoop earrings have transcended trend status to become a wardrobe staple. In Gurgaon, we're seeing demand for hoops in various sizes and finishes — from classic smooth polished to twisted, hammered, and diamond-accented versions. Hollow construction keeps them lightweight and comfortable for all-day wear, while 22K hallmark gold ensures lasting value.

9 Tennis Bracelets & Chains

The diamond tennis bracelet has evolved from a special occasion piece to an everyday essential. Tennis chains (diamond-studded necklaces in a continuous setting) are the newer iteration of this classic. In Gurgaon, we're seeing strong demand for both — worn individually as statement pieces or layered for maximum impact. Our tennis collection features VS clarity diamonds in 18K gold, starting from ₹75,000 for bracelets and ₹1.5 lakh for chains.

10 Sustainable Lab-Grown Diamonds

Environmental consciousness is influencing jewellery choices in Gurgaon. Lab-grown diamonds — chemically and optically identical to natural diamonds but created in controlled environments — offer the same beauty at 60-70% lower prices. While natural diamonds remain the preferred choice for engagements and heirloom pieces, lab-grown options are gaining traction for fashion jewellery and everyday wear. At Auric Jewels, we offer both natural and lab-grown certified diamonds, empowering you to choose what aligns with your values and budget.

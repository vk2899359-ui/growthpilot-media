Diamond Shapes for Solitaire Rings

The shape of your diamond defines the character of your ring. At Auric Jewels, we offer solitaire rings in Gurgaon across all popular diamond shapes:

Round Brilliant

The most popular diamond shape, accounting for over 60% of all solitaire purchases. The 57/58 facets are mathematically optimised for maximum fire and brilliance. If you want the most sparkle possible, round brilliant is the choice.

Princess Cut

A square shape with sharp corners and exceptional brilliance. The princess cut offers a modern, geometric aesthetic while maintaining the fire of a round diamond. Often more affordable per carat than round brilliants.

Cushion Cut

A square or rectangular shape with rounded corners, resembling a pillow. Cushion cuts have a vintage, romantic quality and are one of the fastest-growing choices for engagement rings in Gurgaon in 2025.

Oval Cut

An elongated shape that creates the illusion of a larger diamond and slender fingers. Oval solitaires are elegant, unique, and excellent value — often appearing 15-20% larger than round diamonds of the same carat weight.

Emerald Cut

A rectangular shape with step-cut facets that create a hall-of-mirrors effect. Emerald cuts are the choice of the sophisticated buyer who values understated elegance over maximum sparkle.

Settings & Metals

The setting holds your diamond and defines the ring's overall design. At Auric Jewels, every setting is crafted to secure your stone perfectly while maximising its visual impact.

Classic Prong (4 or 6 prong): The most popular setting, allowing maximum light to enter the diamond for unmatched brilliance. Six prongs offer extra security; four prongs show more of the stone.

Bezel Setting: A metal rim surrounds the diamond completely, offering maximum protection. Ideal for active lifestyles and daily-wear solitaires.

Halo Setting: A circle of smaller diamonds surrounds the centre stone, creating a larger and more brilliant appearance.

Metal Options: Choose from 18K White Gold (most popular for solitaires), 18K Yellow Gold (classic warmth), 18K Rose Gold (romantic and trendy), or Platinum 950 (the ultimate in durability and prestige).

Understanding Diamond Quality: The 4Cs

Making an informed purchase starts with understanding the 4Cs of diamonds — the universal standard for evaluating diamond quality:

Cut

The most important factor in a diamond's beauty. Cut determines how well a diamond reflects light, creating that coveted sparkle and fire. Our gemologists recommend prioritising cut quality above all other factors. At Auric Jewels, we stock diamonds rated Good to Excellent on the GIA cut scale.

Clarity

Clarity measures the presence of natural imperfections called inclusions. Grades range from FL (Flawless) to I3 (Included). For the best value in diamond jewellery in Gurgaon, we recommend VS1-VS2 clarity — eye-clean diamonds at a fraction of the cost of flawless stones.

Colour

Diamond colour grades range from D (colourless) to Z (light yellow). Grades D-F are considered colourless, while G-J offer excellent value — appearing white to the naked eye while being significantly more affordable.

Carat Weight

Carat measures a diamond's weight, not size. Two diamonds of the same carat weight can appear different sizes based on their cut and proportions. Our gemologists help you understand how carat weight interacts with the other Cs to maximise your diamond's visual impact.
